How to Test Notifications

  1. Click "Enable Push Notifications"
  2. Allow notifications in the browser prompt
  3. Copy the FCM token that appears
  4. Go to Firebase Console
  5. Navigate to Engage > Cloud Messaging
  6. Click "Send your first message"
  7. Paste the token as the target
  8. Send a test notification!
