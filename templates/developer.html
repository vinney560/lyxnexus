About LyxNexus

LyxNexus is a simple yet powerful class hub designed to keep everything in one place — from announcements and assignments to topics and class discussions. It provides educators and students with a centralized platform for managing educational content and fostering collaborative learning environments.

Vision & Philosophy

LyxNexus is not just a platform — it's a vision to provide a seamless, lightweight, and intuitive educational experience. I created this platform to give educators and students a unified space where learning materials, discussions, and assignments can be easily accessed and managed. The focus is on simplicity, accessibility, and creating an environment that enhances the educational journey.

Technologies Used

  • Backend: Flask (Python), Jinja2, JWT, WTForms, SQLite
  • Frontend: TailwindCSS, responsive UI, SVG icons, glass morphism effects
  • Authentication: Flask-Login, password hashing, role-based access
  • Security: CSRF protection, rate limiting, session control
  • Deployment: Hosted on Render, SEO with sitemap.xml and robots.txt
